<p class="p1">Join hundreds of young adults from all corners of Australia in an inspiring four-day retreat known as Converge. Taking place at the beautiful Stuarts Point Convention Centre in NSW, Converge is a spirit-filled gathering organised annually by the Australian Union Conference (AUC).</p>
<p class="p1">Over the course of this event, you'll have the opportunity to participate in powerful worship sessions, enlightening workshops, exhilarating beach visits, and engaging social activities. The underlying themes of our event invite you to become more than just a fan of Jesus, but a devoted follower, and to explore ways to neighbour well in a diverse world.</p>
<p class="p1">The passion and dedication of our young attendees truly make Converge a special experience. As AUC youth director Pastor Jeffrey Parker puts it, "The Adventist church is in very good hands with these young adults as our present and future leaders." This event is not just about growth in numbers, but growth in spiritual leadership and missional living.</p>
<p class="p1">Come to Converge, step up, live for Jesus, and be part of this vibrant, faith-filled community.</p>
