<h4><span style="color: #ff9900;">Access &amp; Registration</span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Entry to the meeting rooms is through the main office located at<span> </span><strong>289 Maroondah Highway</strong>. The office opens at<span> </span><strong>8:30 am</strong>.</li>
<li>All visitors must<span> </span><strong>register at reception</strong><span> </span>before proceeding to their designated meeting rooms. Our receptionist,<span> </span><strong>Adelle</strong>, will assist you with signing in and, if available, provide you with a<span> </span><strong>swipe card</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span style="color: #ff9900;">Swipe Card Access</span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The swipe card grants access to all office entry points, including the carparks.</li>
<li>If you do not have a swipe card and leave the premises during the day, you must re-enter via<span> </span><strong>reception at 289</strong>. Please<span> </span><strong>do not use the 287 entrance</strong>, as it is only accessible with a swipe card and cannot be opened manually.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span style="color: #ff9900;">For Conference Presidents</span></h4>
<ul>
<li>A friendly reminder to<span> </span><strong>bring your personal swipe card</strong><span> </span>with you.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span style="color: #ff9900;">Before You Leave</span></h4>
<ul>
<li>On the final day of your advisory, please return your swipe card to<span> </span><strong>reception</strong><span> </span>or to the<span> </span><strong>PA responsible for your advisory group</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="color: #ff9900;">Accommodation Details</span></h3>
<p>All individuals who request accommodation have three choices:</p>
<p><span style="color: #333300;"><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">Choice one:</span><br /></strong></span><strong>Quality Hotel Manor</strong><br />669 Maroondah Highway, Mitcham<br />Phone: (03) 9872 4200 (24 hour number)<br />Email: <a href="mailto:info@qualityhotelmanor.com.au">info@qualityhotelmanor.com.au</a></p>
<p><strong>Please note:<br /></strong> - Your booking includes the option to order a hot breakfast.<strong><br /></strong> - Reception closes at <strong>11:00 pm</strong>. If you expect to arrive after <strong>10:30 pm</strong>, you must contact the hotel in advance to receive instructions for after-hours check-in.</p>
<p><span style="color: #333300;"><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">Choice two:</span><br /></strong></span><strong>The Canterbury International</strong><br />326 Canterbury Rd, Forest Hill VIC 3131<br />Phone: (03) 98784111<span style="color: #333300;"><br /></span></p>
<p><strong>Please note:</strong><br />The hotels check in time is 2pm and check out time is 10am with 24-hour reception staff on duty.</p>
<p><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>Choice three:</strong></span><br /><strong>Book your accommodation</strong>.  The AUC will reimburse $140 per night's stay toward your cost.<span style="color: #333300;"></span></p>
<p><span>If your plans change and you no longer require accommodation, please notify your</span><span> host department’s</span><span> Administrative Assistant and contact the hotel directly.</span></p>
<p><span>If your flight arrival time changes and you need to travel the night before, please contact the hotel directly to adjust your booking, and advise your </span><span>host department’s</span><span> Administrative Assistant.</span><span></span></p>
