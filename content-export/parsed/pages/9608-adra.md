<h2>Emergency Relief</h2>
<p>ADRA Australia operates community programs nationwide. Our emergency relief services are available to assist families and individuals who are experiencing hardship and who would otherwise fall through the cracks. Our emergency relief includes crisis food hampers, access to women’s shelters, and community meals.</p>
