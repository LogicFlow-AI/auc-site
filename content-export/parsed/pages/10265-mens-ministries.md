<p>Men have been entrusted with a unique responsibility to lead by example, both in their family life and in their communities. Our Men's Ministry is dedicated to equipping men with the resources and tools they need to fulfil this sacred duty.</p>
<p>At the heart of our Men's Ministry is the belief that men have an essential role to play in society as loving husbands, dedicated fathers, and servant-minded leaders. We understand that as men grow in maturity and raise a family, they have an incredibly important role to play as role models in the lives of their children.</p>
<p>By showing loving leadership in the home, fathers are able to give their children a glimpse of the love, patience, compassion, and forgiveness of God, our Heavenly Father. We believe that this is the foundation upon which strong families and communities are built.</p>
<p>We understand that leadership takes practical, consistent, positive living, not just lip service when people are looking. It is free and anonymous.</span><span></span></p>
