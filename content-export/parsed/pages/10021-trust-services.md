<p><span>Trust Services is a ministry within the Australian Union Conference of the Seventh-day Adventist Church that aims to provide guidance and assistance to individuals seeking to make informed decisions about their financial planning and estate management. Our focus is on helping you align your faith, values, and convictions with your financial planning, ensuring that you honour God in all aspects of your life.</span></p>
<p><span>We envision a community of informed and responsible individuals who actively engage in sound financial planning and stewardship, guided by their faith in Jesus Christ.</span></p>[/et_pb_text][et_pb_text _builder_version="4.19.5" _module_preset="default" text_font="Montserrat|300|||||||" text_font_size="16px" text_line_height="1.6em" header_2_font="Montserrat|700||on|||||" header_2_text_color="#7a7a7a" header_2_font_size="36px" background_color="#ffffff" custom_padding="50px|50px|50px|50px|true|true" header_2_font_size_tablet="36px" header_2_font_size_phone="20px" header_2_font_size_last_edited="on|phone" border_width_top="1px" border_color_top="#ff8200" box_shadow_style="preset3" box_shadow_color="rgba(0,0,0,0.08)" global_colors_info="{}"]<h2><strong>Key Services and Initiatives</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Estate Planning: Providing guidance on creating a comprehensive estate plan that reflects your values and ensures the proper distribution of your assets.</li>
<li>Wills: Assisting with the preparation of wills to ensure that your wishes are honored and your loved ones are cared for.</li>
<li>Planned Gifts: Offering information on various planned giving options, such as bequests, annuities, and trusts, that can benefit both you and the Church.</li>
<li>Expert Consultation: Connecting you with local experts who can provide personalized assistance in aligning your financial planning with your faith and values.</li>
<li>Educational Resources: Providing access to a wealth of information and resources to help you make informed decisions about your financial future.</li>
