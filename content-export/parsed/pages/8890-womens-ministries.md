box_shadow_color="rgba(0,0,0,0.08)" global_colors_info="{}"]<h2>About Womens Ministry</h2>
<p>The vision of Adventist Women's Ministries is to know Jesus passionately and to serve and disciple other women. We are committed to encouraging, challenging, equipping and nurturing Seventh-day Adventist women as they do their part in carrying the Gospel message to the world. </p>
<p>The Seventh-day Adventist Church in Australia believes that every woman has God-given potential and we want to empower them, encourage them and equip them to confidently share their faith in whichever context they find themselves in. </p>
<p>Our mission is in the larger sense to all Christians<span>—</span>that of uplifting Christ in the Church and in the world. But more specifically, we are called to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Nurture<span>—</span>Reach Up to God</li>
<li>Empower<span>—</span>Reach Across with God</li>
<li>Outreach<span>—</span>Reach Out with God</li>
</ul>
