<p><span>The Adventist message of hope is rooted in a deep understanding of the Bible and the teachings of Jesus. The Communications Department of the Seventh-day Adventist Church plays a vital role in sharing this message with the world. Through the use of various platforms, including phone apps, websites, social media, video programming, and more, the department provides the tools necessary for church institutions and members to effectively and invitingly communicate the Adventist message to audiences both within and outside of the church.
