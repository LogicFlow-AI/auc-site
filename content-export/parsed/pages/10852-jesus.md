of mortal existence as Jesus Christ</span>. It is through Him, the Anointed One, that the universe found its genesis, the divine character of the Creator was unveiled, the redemption of mankind was attained, and judgement cast upon the world. Remaining at all times genuinely divine, He assumed the mantle of humanity, embodying the truth of Christ. Conceived by the sacred breath of the Holy Spirit and given birth by the Virgin Mary, He trod the earth and weathered temptation just as any man, yet He alone mirrored the perfect righteousness and love of God.</p>
<p>His miracles, like dazzling rays of divine light piercing the veil of our earthly realm, bore testament to the Almighty's power and affirmed His identity as the long-awaited Messiah.
