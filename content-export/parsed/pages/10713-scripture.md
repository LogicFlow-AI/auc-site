<h1>Scripture</h1>
<p>The Holy Scriptures, Old and New Testaments, are the written Word of God, given by divine inspiration. The inspired authors spoke and wrote as they were moved by the Holy Spirit. <span>Perusing the divine scriptures is akin to gazing upon a grand masterpiece; a cursory glance reveals but a glimpse of its profound beauty. The deep study of the Bible, akin to examining each brushstroke, shade, and texture, unveils the depth of divine wisdom. </span>
