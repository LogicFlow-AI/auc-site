<div class="xdj266r x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs">Join the global initiative to raise awareness and advocate for the end of violence around the world. enditnow is an initiative by the Department of Women’s Ministries of the Seventh-day Adventist Church and ADRA (Adventist Development and Relief Agency) that seeks to end violence against women, men and children. It aims to mobilize Seventh-day Adventists around the world and incite other community groups to join in to resolve this worldwide issue.</div>
<div></div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s">Help Out with a ‘Go Bag’</div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s"><br class="html-br" />Imagine the relief and dignity you can offer to someone escaping violence by donating a Domestic Violence Emergency Bag. Your contribution can be the pivotal lifeline that transforms fear into hope, providing immediate security and comfort during their darkest hour. Your generosity has the power to change lives when it matters most.</div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s">Individual item donations are welcome too.</div>
<div></div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s">Donate to your local Conference by July 31st, 2025, or bring items along to the Rally. Bags will be distributed at local missions and organisations</div>
