<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Faith FM is inviting supporters to make tax-deductible donations during the month of June through a partnership with Adventist Media’s AMN Cultural Trust.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The Christian radio network currently reaches more than 2.7 million listeners each month—over 10 per cent of Australia’s population—through more than 350 broadcast towers. In 2023, Faith FM launched 78 new sites, its largest expansion to date.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Many listeners are tuning in for spiritual guidance and connecting with local churches for further support. The station has also been focused on producing more local content, expanding community engagement and running outreach events such as community dinners across Australia.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“Looking ahead, God has put a big vision on the hearts of our Faith FM team to pray for 1 million meaningful engagements with listeners in the next few years,” said Faith FM station manager Michael Engelbrecht. “That’s 100 times where we are today.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
