<h4><strong>Important Information</strong></h4>
Welcome to our Australian-wide, 18+ festival. A place to hang with Jesus and your mates!

Please provide a working and regularly checked contact number and email in the form below. All ticket and event information will be sent via email.

There are some awesome entry deals throughout the year, be sure to get in before the price increase! (closing dates will be listed)

Join the Converge Facebook Group here: https://www.facebook.com/groups/YMAConverge

For updates and more check out our Instagram page: @youthministriesaustralia

Other important info:
- Contact Yarra Adventure and Holiday Park (Stuarts Point Convention Centre) directly to book a Cabin or hire a tent
- You must be at least 18 by February 9th, 2024 to attend
- Meal Tickets close on Tuesday the 15th Jan 2024
- Refunds close on December 31st, 2023 (see further information at the end of your application)

We look forward to seeing you there!
