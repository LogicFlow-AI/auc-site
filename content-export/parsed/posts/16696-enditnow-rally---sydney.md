<div class="xdj266r x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs">enditnow® is a global initiative to raise awareness and advocate for the end of violence around the world.</div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s">It aims to mobilise Seventh-day Adventists around the world and invites other community groups to join in to resolve this worldwide issue.</div>
<div></div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s">THE RALLY</div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s">We'll be gathering to discuss violence and the importance of safe spaces for those needing them. Join us and special guests for this public event.</div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s">We'll be providing a Sausage Sizzle afterwards</div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s"></div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s">WHAT TO DO</div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s">We're running a Survival Pack Drive. The Adventist Church in Australia is partnering with the Adventist Development and Relief Agency (ADRA) to provide emergency “Go Bags” for people experiencing domestic violence.</div>
<div></div>
<div class="x14z9mp xat24cr x1lziwak x1vvkbs xtlvy1s">The Go Bags will be distributed during or after EnditNow Emphasis Day, which encourages churches across Australia to join the global movement to end violence against women, men and children.</div>
