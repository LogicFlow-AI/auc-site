<div dir="auto">Notice is hereby given that the regular constituency meeting of the Australian Union Conference (AUC) of the Seventh-day Adventist Church will be convened from August 31 to September 4, 2025, at the Parkroyal Monash Melbourne, 872 Blackburn Road, Clayton, Melbourne, Victoria.</div>
<div dir="auto"></div>
</div>
<div class="x11i5rnm xat24cr x1mh8g0r x1vvkbs xtlvy1s x126k92a">
<div dir="auto">The business of this constituency meeting will be in accordance with the constitution. Such business will include the election of leadership, reports, proposed changes to the constitution and will also address a discussion on the ordination of pastors within the Australian Union Conference. The constituency meeting will commence at 7pm on Sunday, August 31. Registration will take place from 3pm on August 31 and is necessary prior to the commencement time.</div>
<div dir="auto"></div>
