<strong>THE FUTURE OF HEALTH</strong>

Calling all health leaders, professionals and enthusiasts!  You’re invited to join us on the stunning Gold Coast for the ELIA Wellness Summit on 24-26 May 2024.

Discover how Lifestyle Medicine can transform the body, mind and spirit and empower your local Health Ministry. Bring your health team, friends and colleagues for an inspiring and thought-provoking event filled with the latest lifestyle medicine and evidence-based whole-person health presentations and networking opportunities. Be inspired to bring health, healing and hope to your local community! Be uplifted by our special Sabbath service as we share the healing message of health and how one small step can make an incredible impact in the lives of those searching for hope.

When: 24 - 26 May 2024

Where: Mantra Legends Hotel, Surfers Paradise

Hosts: Dr Geraldine Przybylko (ELIA Wellness Executive Director and SPD Health Strategy Leader) and Prof Darren Morton
