<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Around 250 young people gathered at Gilson College Community Church in Victoria for a night of praise and testimony on September 17.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Hosted by the Australian Union Conference (AUC) youth department’s official publication&nbsp;<em>My&nbsp;Edge</em>&nbsp;magazine, the event was also live-streamed online to make it accessible for those who couldn’t attend in person.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The event featured young adult speakers Pauline Escano and Janelle Bocala, who shared their testimonies, highlighting the ways that God has worked in their lives.&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Another highlight of the event was the special items performed by Le Quest and Gilson College Community Church band throughout the night.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For attendee Ellen Holman from Geelong Seventh-day Adventist Church, the highlight of the night was “hearing the touching and inspirational testimonies from Janelle and Pauline”.  </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“It was a great opportunity to worship with other young adults. I am looking forward to the next Praise and Testimony night,” she added.&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Social media coordinator for&nbsp;<em>My Edge</em>&nbsp;magazine&nbsp;Kapungwe&nbsp;Besa&nbsp;said, “It was such a blessing seeing&nbsp;that many&nbsp;youth come out on a Friday night to worship together. The Holy Spirit was&nbsp;definitely&nbsp;present in the room.&nbsp;Many tears were shed, including my own. We are excited to bring the event to Avondale next week.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>My Edge</em> magazine plans to host their next Praise and Testimony night at Avondale University on October 11 at 7pm, in collaboration with Salt. </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
