Join us on the Trip from Australia to the International Camporee 2024 in Gillette, Wyoming (USA).

The international camporee is a great opportunity to meet Pathfinders from around the world. The 2024 Believe the Promise Camporee will highlight the exciting story of Moses and Miriam in an exciting production each evening as well as offer various honours and activities that Pathfinders from around the world will enjoy.
<h3>Australian Union Conference (AUC) Trip Registration</h3>
This link is set up for individuals to register, not clubs. It is to give your notice of intention to attend and secure your spot with a deposit of $200. You will have until January 17th, 2024 to change your mind and obtain a refund. After January 17th, 2024 no refunds will be given.

If you are under 18, you will need a guardian. <a href="https://events.adventist.org.au/ew/app/registration/index.html?e=10899&amp;fbclid=IwAR0ZhpajUd5IM6Gi1cXSHXQ_6U1Byyd1uDizI2OuiqbmoCnP9D_Iw2oOr7U">Click here.</a>
