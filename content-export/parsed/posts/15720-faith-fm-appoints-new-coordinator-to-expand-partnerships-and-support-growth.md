<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><a href="https://faithfm.com.au/">Faith FM</a> has appointed Pastor Benjamin Ng as its new fundraising and marketing coordinator. Currently serving as pastor of <a href="https://alburyadventist.church/">Albury Seventh-day Adventist Church</a>, in NSW, he will commence his new role in early May.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>According to the Faith FM team, the network reaches over 2.7 million monthly listeners—many of whom are turning to the radio for spiritual guidance and increasingly connecting with local churches for ongoing support and community.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Pastor Ng’s role will focus on&nbsp;developing partnerships and fundraising strategies to support Faith FM’s continued growth.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Faith FM&nbsp;content and promotions manager Pastor Robbie Berghan highlighted the significance of the role:&nbsp;“One of the top priorities from our strategic planning session at last year’s Amplify event was strengthening our focus on fundraising and partnerships. This is vital not only to sustain our current operations but also to enable Faith FM’s growth as we pursue our vision of reaching Australia with the life-changing gospel. We’re excited to welcome Pastor Ng to this important role.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Pastor Ng expressed enthusiasm for the new role,&nbsp;“I’m excited to be part of a ministry that impacts so many lives. I look forward to building partnerships that will help extend Faith FM’s reach.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The new fundraising and marketing coordinator brings experience in both&nbsp;pastoral ministry and fundraising. Before his role in Albury,&nbsp;Pastor Ng served with the Malaysia Mission, where he worked in pastoral ministry, led fundraising initiatives, trained Bible workers and taught at the&nbsp;SALT Bible School.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Faith FM&nbsp;station manager Michael Engelbrecht welcomed the appointment, “We’re delighted Pastor Ng is joining our team. He brings a genuine passion and experience that will help us better serve our listeners and grow our ministry.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Australian Union Conference president Pastor Terry Johnson added, “Faith FM’s reach continues to grow, and having Pastor Ng on board will help us strengthen the partnerships needed to sustain and expand this important ministry.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Pastor Ng is married to Hui Yee and they have three children.</p>
